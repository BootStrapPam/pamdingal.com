Checklist Before Publishing Your First Business Website

Before you launch your website, review these essentials to avoid common mistakes and make a stronger first impression.

Launch checklist

  • Homepage headline clearly states what you offer
  • Contact information is visible and clickable
  • Service descriptions are specific and easy to scan
  • Images are clear and optimized for fast loading
  • Website works properly on mobile
  • Social links and buttons are functional

Bonus tip

Add one trust signal on the homepage, like sample work, testimonials, or a clear process. It helps visitors decide faster.
