Canva Design Tips for Non-Designers

You do not need a design degree to create polished graphics. You just need structure, consistency, and a few smart rules.

1. Use a tight color palette

Pick one primary color, one supporting color, and one neutral. Too many colors can make a design look unprofessional.

2. Limit your fonts

Use one font for headings and one for body text. This keeps your posts, brochures, and slides visually consistent.

3. Prioritize spacing

Leave breathing room around text blocks and images. Clean spacing instantly improves design quality.

4. Build reusable templates

Create a few branded templates for social posts, stories, and presentation slides so your content stays consistent and faster to produce.
